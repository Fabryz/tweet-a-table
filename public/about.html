What

Tweet-a-table is a real-time ranking of tweets about key sporting events. In the near future we plan to extend the logic to non-sport topics (such as elections, word of mouth about automotive brands, etc.).

How

The idea is born during the third edition of H-ack2012, an hackaton event where H-Farm and H-Art developers meet to think, plan and build applications in a collaborative way. This time the main theme was sport, with particular attention to European Football Championship and Olympic Games: four teams of geeks coded hard from nine o'clock in the morning until 2pm to build innovative applications.
